About Lipscomb

Lipscomb University is a private Christian liberal arts institution located in Nashville, Tennessee. Founded in 1891, it has a rich history of academic excellence and spiritual growth.

Academics

Lipscomb offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines, including business, education, engineering, and the arts and sciences.
